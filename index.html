iOS app + computer vision pipeline to automate school carpool logistics.

InsurAI

Healthcare insurance risk prediction with structured data + facial analysis.

Developed gradient-boosted models for insurance risk and integrated a CNN-based facial analysis module to explore correlations with health metrics. Packaged as an iOS app with an intuitive UI and geospatial features via Apple Maps.

Tokenization for Generative Molecule Models

Custom tokenizer for SMILES strings with improved compression and downstream performance.

Implemented trie-based tokenization and entropy-guided token selection for chemical sequences. Evaluated tokenizers on property prediction tasks and clustering metrics, showing gains over standard baselines.
